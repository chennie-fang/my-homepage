After my investigation, I found that many living creatures have become hermaphrodites because of the effects of chemical pollution, and the future appearance of human beings may also be hermaphrodites. In the Jewish version of the Bible, Adam, the ancestor of mankind, was a hermaphrodite who became a male after eating the forbidden fruit. In every time period, most of the androgynous imagery has helped society to cross the falling fence of the gender binary. In this time period there is still a strong gender binary, so I imagined a world where Adam did not eat the forbidden fruit. Through this animation I hope that people can use this animation to think about whether or not the current gender binary world is the right one.
