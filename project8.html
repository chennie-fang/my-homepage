Through Touch Designer combined with Kinect , we simulate players immersing themselves in a flower field, where a self-evolving plant life system emerges. The plant tissues that grow on the users exhibit the shape of roots and stems, with characteristics resembling veins. Pulsating orchids, resembling breathing lungs, emerge from the users' bodies. The effects of the plants seamlessly blend into the natural background as users sway their limbs. showcasing the evolutionary direction where humans choose to coexist with nature in the future.


通过 Touch Designer 与 Kinect 的结合，让玩家沉浸在一片花田中，在那里出现了一个自我进化的植物生命系统，它会生长在玩家的身上的，植物组织呈现出根和茎的形状，具有类似脉络的特征。玩家的身体上还会冒出脉动的兰花，就像会呼吸的肺，当玩家摇摆肢体时，植物的效果会与自然的背景融合，展示了未来的人类会与植物共存并进化。
